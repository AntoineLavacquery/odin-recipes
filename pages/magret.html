Magret de Canard Recipe

This easy yet impressive recipe makes a stylish main course for a special occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 2 duck breasts
  • Salt and pepper
  • A knob of butter

Instructions:

  1. Use a very sharp knife to score the skin on each duck breast, cutting 1â„2" diamonds across the entire surface.
  2. Season all over with salt and pepper.
  3. Add the duck breasts to the pan, skin side down, and cook for 5 to 6 minutes, until plenty of fat has rendered and the skin is a deep golden brown.
  4. Flip the breasts and cook for another 8-10 minutes for medium-rare (rosy in the middle), or longer if you want it cooked more.
