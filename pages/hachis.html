Hachis Parmentier Recipe

Ingredients: - 500g of ground beef - 1 onion - Garlic - Gravy - Vegetables - Worcestershire sauce - Salt and pepper - Potatoes **Instructions:** 1. In a large skillet, cook the beef and onion over medium heat until the meat is no longer pink, breaking the meat into crumbles. 2. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute longer. 3. Drain the mixture. 4. Stir in the gravy, vegetables, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper. 5. Transfer the mixture to a greased 1-1/2-qt. baking dish. 6. Spread mashed potatoes over the top. 7.
